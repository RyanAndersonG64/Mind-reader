Welcome to my Birdwatching page!

My community welcomes all birdwatchers, beginners and experts alike. Feel free to browse my photos and favorite birdwatching locations, or visit the discussion boards to share your own photos as well as exchange tips and advice with others.

If you would like to get into birdwatching but are unsure how, make sure to check out the pinned "Getting Started" discussion thread. We have many friendly users on this site who are willing to help!
